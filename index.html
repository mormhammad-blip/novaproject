Welcome to Nova

At Nova, we believe that every dish should tell a story—one painted with the purest, most vibrant colors nature has to offer. Our mission is to bring you 100% natural, plant-based pigments that transform your culinary creations into edible masterpieces.

Why You’ll Love Our Colors

Pure & Safe: Harvested from Lebanese herbs and flowers, our powders contain no artificial additives or chemicals.

Vibrant & Versatile: Instantly dissolve in frostings, batters, beverages, or yogurt to achieve stunning hues.

Eco-Friendly: Sustainably sourced, supporting local farmers while caring for the planet.
